Company Profile

Y.T. Rubber (YTRC) was established in 2008 to supply natural rubber to the tire factories of the Yokohama Rubber Group. The factory is located in Suratthani Province, southern Thailand — one of the main rubber-producing areas globally.

Thailand produces approximately 4.8 million tons of natural rubber annually, making it the largest producer in the world. About two-thirds of this is produced in the south, with Surat Thani being a key area.

Y.T. Rubber sources and processes raw rubber and supplies it to Yokohama’s tire manufacturing bases in Thailand, Japan, the USA, China, the Philippines, and India — serving a wide range of high-performance tire products.

As part of the Yokohama Group, we are certified with ISO9001, ISO14001, and FSC COC, committed to environmental contribution.
